Duel Monsters VR
Lead Developer
Duel Monsters VR is a VR fan-game of Yu-Gi-Oh!

The game was architected to have one central, social lobby with server-authoritative matches. Players could socialize in the lobby, challenge each other to duels, and join guilds. There is a website for players to manage their account, build customized decks, and check rankings.

We deployed this game for Oculus CV1 and Steam VR for users to play 1v1, 2v2, and free-for-all duels on a variety of maps and environments. Players can customize their avatars and duel with either pre-built character decks or use their own customized deck.

We also held occasional tournaments and have a thriving Discord community of over 40,000 members.




Decibel
Lead Developer & Game Director
Decibel was my team's submission for Oculus Launch Pad 2020. Oculus Launch Pad was originally a program where, each year, 100 XR professionals were selected to create VR prototpes or vertical slice experiences for Meta VR headsets.

Decibel is a VR single-player, rhythm-shooter game where players could fight a hydra in increasing levels of difficulty while shooting to the beats following a custom-made song.

Decibel was developed and deployed to run on Meta Quest 1, meaning optimization and performance were crucial to our design and development. We pushed the limits to make sure the player could experience a myriad of engaging and breath-taking visual effects.

Soul Drop
VR Developer
Soul Drop was my team's submission for Oculus Launch Pad 2018. Oculus Launch Pad was originally a program where, each year, 100 XR professionals were selected to create VR prototpes or vertical slice experiences for Meta VR headsets.

Soul Drop was a multiplayer RPG built for Oculus CV1. Players could physically battle against fantasy monsters together. Players could choose among 3 weapons with special abilities, and work with their teammates to utilize cooperative abilities. There was a small mini-boss and main boss to fight as well as a parkour "jump quest" platforming area.
