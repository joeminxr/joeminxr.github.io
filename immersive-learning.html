The Project
Duel Monsters VR was a VR fan-game of Yu-Gi-Oh!

We deployed this game for Oculus CV1 and Steam VR for users to play 1v1, 2v2, and free-for-all duels with options for pre-made and custom decks. Users could customize their avatars.

They could talk to each other in the lobby, challenge each other to duels, and join guilds. We also held occasional tournaments and had a thriving Discord community of over 40,000 members.
